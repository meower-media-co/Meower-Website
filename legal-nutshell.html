Meower Legal (In a nutshell)

Last updated 11 June 2023


Important Notice

This page is a summary of Meower's legal page. It is not a substitute. It is encouraged that you read and understand the legal page during account setup.


Basic Rules

You should always follow these rules when using Meower. Failing to do so can result in moderator action, such as kicks, bans, or terminations.


If you're under 13, we'll need you to get permission from a parent or legal guardian before you can sign up!


Always make sure you respect others' rights and privacy. Don't share their personal info without permission.


Always keep any posts, comments, messages or threads "Rated E for everyone" friendly, and never share pornographic or vulgar content.


Always respect the will of other users on Meower. For example, if someone asks you to delete a parody account that involves them, you should always do so.


Always respect intellectual property rights. No copyrighted content should be shared unless you have explicit written permission to share said content.


Never spam on Meower. This includes making multiple posts in a short period of time.


Don't scam people on Meower. This includes links that steal people's information (phishing).


Don't impersonate people on Meower. All parody accounts should state that they are parodies in their quote or username.


Don't harass people on Meower. Any harassment will result in a ban.


Your Privacy

We will never collect any personal information, such as real names, dates of birth, gender info, or location data.


When you use Meower, your IP address will be shared with IPHub for moderation purposes. This is to help prevent ban evasion with VPNs and proxies, as well as preventing certain users from using Meower.


All Meower traffic is passed through Cloudflare to help protect our servers from DDoS attacks and other malicious activity. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available here.


We only collect info we need to help run our service, including your username, a hashed version of your password, quote, profile picture, your posts, comments, messages, and failed account login attempts (for security).


We will notify you of all policy updates and data breaches in the event that they occur.
